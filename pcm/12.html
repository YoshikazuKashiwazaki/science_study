問題1

まず式1 \[ u(x,t)=f(kx-\omega t) \] より, \[ \frac{\partial}{\partial t}u(x,t)=\frac{\partial}{\partial t}f(kx-\omega t) =-\omega f(kx-\omega t) . \] また, \[ \frac{\partial}{\partial x}u(x,t)=\frac{\partial}{\partial x}f(kx-\omega t) =k f(kx-\omega t) . \] よって, \[ \frac{\partial^2}{\partial t^2}u(x,t)=\frac{\partial}{\partial t}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial t}u(x,t)\right) =\omega ^2 f(kx-\omega t). \] また, \[ \frac{\partial^2}{\partial x^2}u(x,t)=\frac{\partial}{\partial x}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial x}u(x,t)\right) =k^2 f(kx-\omega t). \] \[ \therefore \frac{\partial^2}{\partial t^2}u(x,t)-v^2\frac{\partial^2}{\partial x^2}u(x,t) =\omega ^2 f(kx-\omega t)-\left(\frac{\omega}{k}\right)^2 k^2 f(kx-\omega t)=0 . \]
