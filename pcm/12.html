$m=n$のとき, \[ \int_{0}^{L} \Psi_m(x)^{*}\Psi_n(x)dx=\int_{0}^{L} \sqrt\frac{2}{L}\sin \left(\frac{n\pi}{L} x\right) \sqrt\frac{2}{L}\sin \left(\frac{n\pi}{L} x\right)dx\\ =\frac{2}{L} \int_{0}^{L} \sin^2 \left(\frac{n\pi}{L} x\right)dx =\frac{2}{L} \frac{1}{2} \int_{0}^{L} \left(1-\cos\left(\frac{n\pi}{L}\right)\right)dx\\ =\frac{1}{L} \left[x\right]_{0}^{L} - \frac{L}{n\pi}\left[\sin\frac{n\pi}{L}x\right]_{0}^{L}=1-0=1 \]

$m\neq n$のとき, \[ \int_{0}^{L} \Psi_m(x)^{*}\Psi_n(x)dx=\int_{0}^{L} \sqrt\frac{2}{L}\sin \left(\frac{m\pi}{L} x\right) \sqrt\frac{2}{L}\sin \left(\frac{n\pi}{L} x\right)dx\\ =\frac{2}{L}\frac{1}{2} \int_{0}^{L} \left(\cos\frac{(m-n)\pi}{L}x -\cos\frac{(m+n)\pi}{L}x\right)dx =\frac{2}{L} \frac{1}{2} \int_{0}^{L} \left(1-\cos\left(\frac{n\pi}{L}\right)\right)dx\\ =\frac{1}{L} \left[x\right]_{0}^{L} - \frac{L}{n\pi}\left[\sin\frac{n\pi}{L}x\right]_{0}^{L}=1-0=1 \]

微分可能な関数は連続である. しかし, 連続な関数は微分可能とは限らない.

たとえば, $y=|x|$は$x=0$で連続であるが, $x=0$で微分可能ではない.
