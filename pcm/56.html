問題1

天井から鉛直下向きを正に$x$軸をとり, 水平方向右向きを正として$y$軸をとる. 原点は天井に近い錘の紐が天井に接する位置にとる.
天井に近いほうの錘が$x$軸と反時計回りになす角度を$\theta_{1}$, 天井から遠い方の錘が同様になす角を$\theta_{2}$とする.
各紐にはそれぞれ$T_{1}$, $T_{2}$の張力が働くとする.
このとき, 天井から近い方と遠い方の錘に働く力$\pmb F_{1}$, $\pmb F_{2}$は次のようにベクトルで示せる. \[ \pmb F_{1}=(mg-T_{1}\cos\theta_{1}+T_{2}\cos\theta_{2}, -T_{1}\sin\theta_{1}+T_{2}\sin\theta_{2})\\ \pmb F_{2}=(mg-T_{2}\cos\theta_{2},T_{2}\sin\theta_{2}) \] 他方, それぞれの錘の$xy$座標を角度$\theta_{1}$,$\theta_{2}$でベクトル表示すると次のようになる. \[ \pmb r_{1}=(l\cos\theta_{1}, l\sin\theta_{1})\\ \pmb r_{2}=(l(\cos\theta_{1}+\cos\theta_{2}), l(\sin\theta_{1}+\sin\theta_{2})) \] 位置ベクトルを時間微分し, 速度ベクトルを得る. \[ \pmb \dot{r}_{1}=(-l\sin\theta_{1}\dot{\theta}_{1}, l\cos\theta_{1})\dot{\theta}_{1})\\ \pmb \dot{r}_{2}=(-l(\sin\theta_{1}\dot{\theta}_{1}+\sin\theta_{2}\dot{\theta}_{2}), l(\cos\theta_{1}\dot{\theta}_{1}}+\cos\theta_{2}\dot{\theta}_{2})) \] さらに, 速度ベクトルを時間微分して加速度ベクトルを得る. \[ \pmb \ddot{r}_{1}=(-l\sin\theta_{1}\dot{\theta}_{1}, l\cos\theta_{1})\dot{\theta}_{1})\\ \pmb \ddot{r}_{2}=(-l(\sin\theta_{1}\dot{\theta}_{1}+\sin\theta_{2}\dot{\theta}_{2}), l(\cos\theta_{1}\dot{\theta}_{1}}+\cos\theta_{2}\dot{\theta}_{2})) \] また, \[ \frac{\partial^2}{\partial x^2}u(x,t)=\frac{\partial}{\partial x}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial x}u(x,t)\right) =k^2 f(kx-\omega t). \] \[ \therefore \frac{\partial^2}{\partial t^2}u(x,t)-v^2\frac{\partial^2}{\partial x^2}u(x,t) =\omega ^2 f(kx-\omega t)-\left(\frac{\omega}{k}\right)^2 k^2 f(kx-\omega t)=0 . \]

2(a)
$m=n$のとき, \[ \int_{0}^{L} \Psi_m(x)^{*}\Psi_n(x)dx=\int_{0}^{L} \sqrt\frac{2}{L}\sin \left(\frac{n\pi}{L} x\right) \sqrt\frac{2}{L}\sin \left(\frac{n\pi}{L} x\right)dx\\ =\frac{2}{L} \int_{0}^{L} \sin^2 \left(\frac{n\pi}{L} x\right)dx =\frac{2}{L} \frac{1}{2} \int_{0}^{L} \left(1-\cos\frac{2n\pi}{L}\right)dx\\ =\frac{1}{L} \left[x\right]_{0}^{L} - \frac{L}{2n\pi}\left[\sin\frac{2n\pi}{L}x\right]_{0}^{L}=1-0=1 \]

$m\neq n$のとき, \[ \int_{0}^{L} \Psi_m(x)^{*}\Psi_n(x)dx=\int_{0}^{L} \sqrt\frac{2}{L}\sin \left(\frac{m\pi}{L} x\right) \sqrt\frac{2}{L}\sin \left(\frac{n\pi}{L} x\right)dx\\ =\frac{2}{L}\frac{1}{2} \int_{0}^{L} \left(\cos\frac{(m-n)\pi}{L}x -\cos\frac{(m+n)\pi}{L}x\right)dx\\ =\frac{1}{L} \left(\frac{L}{(m-n)\pi}\left[-\sin\frac{(m-n)\pi}{L}x\right]_{0}^{L} -\frac{L}{(m+n)\pi}\left[-\sin\frac{(m+n)\pi}{L}x\right]_{0}^{L}\right)\\ =0-0=0 \]

\[ \int_{\frac{1}{4}L}^{\frac{3}{4}L} \left\lvert\Psi_{1}(x)\right\rvert^2 dx=\int_{\frac{1}{4}L}^{\frac{3}{4}L}\left( \sqrt\frac{2}{L}\sin \frac{\pi}{L} x\right)^2 dx\\ =\frac{2}{L} \frac{1}{2} \int_{\frac{1}{4}L}^{\frac{3}{4}L} \left(1-\cos\frac{2\pi}{L}\right)dx\\ =\frac{1}{L} \left[x\right]_{\frac{1}{4}L}^{\frac{3}{4}L} - \frac{L}{2\pi}\left[\sin\frac{2\pi}{L}x\right]_{\frac{1}{4}L}^{\frac{3}{4}L}\\ =\frac{1}{L} \left(\frac{2}{4}L - \frac{L}{\pi}\left(\sin\frac{3\pi}{2}-\sin\frac{\pi}{2}\right)\right)=\frac{1}{2}-\frac{1}{\pi}(-1-1)\\ =\frac{1}{2}+\frac{2}{\pi} \]
